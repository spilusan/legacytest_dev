Competitiveness

Competitive quote situations

The breakdown of the number of competitive quote situations the supplier has been in. A competitiveness quote situation or CQS is where at least one other supplier has been chosen to quote on an RFQ. The number of situations is shown and the number of times the supplier is cheapest or their response is fastest. We also show the average percentage the supplier was cheaper or more expensive comparing to the nearest cheapest or most expensive quote respectively.

Trend

Price sensitivity

Shows the percentage win rate when the supplier is cheapest and is benchmarked against your suppliers and the ShipServ average. We isolate competitive quote situations to where all suppliers quoted within 24 hours of receiving the RFQ, and therefore assume price sensitivity to be a factor in winning the order.

Time sensitivity

Shows the percentage win rate when the supplier is fastest in responding and is benchmarked against your suppliers and the ShipServ average. We isolate competitive quote situations to where suppliers have quoted on all the same items and the total quoted prices are similar. We therefore assume that time sensitivity is a factor in winning the order.

A breakdown of the percentage of times other suppliers have been asked to quote and a breakdown in the table of the most common suppliers being asked to quote against this supplier showing the number of competitive quotes issued, percentage of times that supplier was cheaper and the potential savings from those cheaper quotes.
