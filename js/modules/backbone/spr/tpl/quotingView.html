Response rate

The percentage of RFQs sent to the supplier that received a response of either a quote or a decline to quote. RFQs issued within the last 30 days from the current date that have not been responded to are considered pending. The response rate of the supplier is benchmarked against the response rate of all of your RFQs and also all RFQs issued by all buyers on ShipServ.

Quote variance

This is the percentage of quotes issued where the supplier quoted for exactly what you asked for. If the quote precisely matches the RFQ there is said to be zero variance. For example, a quote with a variance is one where a quote line item description is different to the corresponding RFQ line item description, or where the unit of measure offered was not what was requested or the quantity offered is different to the quantity requested. This is benchmarked against the quotes issued by all your suppliers and against all quotes issued by all suppliers on the ShipServ platform.
