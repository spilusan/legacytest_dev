Direct orders are orders sent to the supplier where there was no preceding RFQ.

Competitive orders are orders where there is a preceding RFQ. Note that the RFQ may have been sent only to this supplier. If an RFQ was sent to the supplier and that supplier went on to win the order but no intervening quote was detected, the quote count assumes that a quote was issued but not via ShipServ.
