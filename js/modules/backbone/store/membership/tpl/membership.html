Membership Fee

An annual membership fee is paid to ShipServ. The fee is per branch/TradeNet ID. It entitles you to use of ShipServ products & various membership benefits which include:

  • Increasing your awareness in front of marine buyers.
  • Generating more new leads for your business.
  • Processing and managing your orders more efficiently and quickly.

It also includes the Supplier Insight Report allowing you to track & measure the performance of your ShipServ Membership.

In addition, membership gives you superior presence on ShipServ Pages, the largest marine search engine, with 3 million visits every year.
