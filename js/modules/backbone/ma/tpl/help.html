How to download ShipServ Onboard to your Smartphone or Tablet

  1. Get GoodReader: Click the button for the GoodReader application corresponding to your device. This application costs about $5 USD from a third party, but it works much better than the free Adobe Reader.
  2. Get the PDF:  Click the button to Login to ShipServ, and follow the onscreen instructions for downloading the PDF.
  3. When the PDF appears on your device, touch the arrow button and select Open In and choose GoodReader. Your document should now come up.

A few helpful hints for navigating ShipServ Onboard on the Smartphone/Tablet using GoodReader

  1. To access the controls, tap the center of the screen.
  2. There is a slide at the bottom of the screen.  Touch the slide control to go to the desired page.
  3. To access the table of contents, touch the symbol of an open book.
    1. Tapping a section name will take you directly to the first page of that section.
    2. For indices:               
      • Tapping the index name will take you to the first page of the index
      • Tapping the blue arrow to the right of the index name will open a second screen with each of the headings in the index
      • When you are viewing the index detail, tapping a Page Number will take you directly to that page.
  4. The back button performs the same function as in a browser.
  5. The magnifying glass triggers a search.  The search is sequential, and the program will indicate its progress in a status bar.
  6. Tap Cancel to terminate a search.  Note: The search is sequential, so if you are looking for a specific term that appears once in the document, it may take some time to find.
  7. There are more functions available in GoodReader which you may find useful.  Tap the Help button at the top right of the screen.

Why GoodReader?

ShipServ has no affiliation with the company that distributes GoodReader. We recommend this application because we have found GoodReader to give the most satisfactory performance when using ShipServ Onboard compared to other readers we have tried.
