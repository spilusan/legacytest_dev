Average quote time

This shows how you compare to other suppliers for the same quotes.

We only count quotes here which meet all these criteria:

  • where at least 2 suppliers issued a quote (one of which has to be you)
  • and where there is an PO issued (which may or may not have been to you)

In the table at the bottom, we divide your quotes up into quote time bands and show your win rate by that band.

We show you what % of your quotes you do in these time bands.

If there are insufficient quotes to show you reliable data, or protect the confidentiality of other suppliers and buyers, we show "n/a".
