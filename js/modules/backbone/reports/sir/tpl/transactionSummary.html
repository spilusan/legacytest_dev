This tab shows the value of orders you received in the time period selected (regardless of when the RFQ was received). So how does this vary from the Sales Funnel tab? The sales funnel tab uses a different definition as it is designed to show sales conversion: it only shows orders connected to RFQs received in the period selected. (Example: period set as month of February. #1 RFQ and its order received February for $50; #2 RFQ received January and its order in February for $40. Sales funnel tab will show $50, Billable tab will show $90).

The information in this tab is used for billing you and corresponds to you invoice. The Sales Funnel data does not match the Billable tab and is not used for billing.
